About Me

I'm an 18-year-old artist from Assam with a bold vision: to create music that feels real, honest, and unforgettable. I believe that a single song can change someone's day, someone’s mind, or someone’s life — and that's the impact I aim to make with my art.

Music is not just something I do — it's the path I'm committed to walking, with full passion and ambition. Every lyric I write and every melody I craft pushes me one step closer to the artist I dream of becoming.

My Music

My first original song, "Tumi Ahiba Ne", marks the starting point of my musical journey. I'm currently working on more projects that explore deeper emotions, stronger storytelling, and a signature style I'm building step by step.

This is just the beginning — and my catalogue will continue to grow with songs that reflect who I am and who I'm becoming.

Ambitions

I aim to rise as a powerful voice from Assam — someone who brings originality, emotion, and identity into the world of music. My ambition is to reach a global audience, perform on major stages, and create songs that stay in people's hearts.

The journey is long, but I’m committed, focused, and driven to turn my dreams into reality.
